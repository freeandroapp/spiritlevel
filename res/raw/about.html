About

A spirit level (clinometer) can be user to check the smoothness of any surface

Features

  • Calibrate option for accuracy
  • Show angle or inclination
  • Sound effects
  • Install on SD
  • Orientation locking
  • Roof pitch
  • Different Sensor Support
  • Share Apps with family and friends
  • More Apps from developer
